How to play

The goal of Mario Memory is to match pairs of cards with the same Mario characters until all cards are successfully matched

  • Players must only flip over two cards at a time
  • Remember the location of cards that have been flipped over to help find matching pairs
  • Players must use their memory and concentration skills to recall the location of cards and match them accordingly
  • The game ends when all pairs of cards have been matched or when the time runs out, you have one minute
